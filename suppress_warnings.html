Suppress Warnings

In any language in Eclipse, simply write @suppress on the line of your warning. Your warning will be suppressed. Even though the warning may still be visible in your file, it is now removed from the Problems View.

Note that you can only suppress warnings, not errors or notes. If your compiler flags an error, you should either fix it or talk to the one who developped the compiler.
